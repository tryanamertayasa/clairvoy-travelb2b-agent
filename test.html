Conclusion Slide 7/7

Next Steps & Recommendations

  1. Defer Partnership: Formally notify the client that the proposal is not viable in its current state.
  2. Request Expanded Scope: To proceed, ask the client to provide a larger portfolio of at least 5-10 high-demand routes or to include additional product types (e.g., hotels, activities).
  3. Quantify Volume Requirements: Clearly communicate that a minimum of ~220 monthly transactions (or equivalent GMV) is required to meet our commercial thresholds.
  4. Clarify Commercial Needs: Request a detailed summary of their preferred payment cycles and pricing models (e.g., NET rates vs. commission).
